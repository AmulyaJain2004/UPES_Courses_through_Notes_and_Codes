About This Repository

This repository was created to provide a centralized and easily accessible location for all my university course materials from UPES.

It is organized by academic year and semester, making it easy to navigate and find specific course notes, assignments, and resources.

The goal is to help students manage their learning materials efficiently and have everything they need in one place.
